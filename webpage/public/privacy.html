Privacy

When you join the AirGap Paste waitlist, we store your email address, consent version, signup timestamp, and campaign-source information to send product and launch updates.

Waitlist data is not public and is not sold. We use it only for AirGap Paste launch communication. You can ask for access or deletion by replying to a future launch email once email delivery is enabled.

This concept-stage site does not currently use analytics cookies. Bot protection may process limited technical information to protect the waitlist form.
