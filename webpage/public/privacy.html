Privacy & cookie notice

AirGap Paste is a prototype-stage product project. This notice explains how the pre-launch website handles personal data and optional analytics.

Before a public commercial launch: publish the legal controller name, postal address and a monitored privacy-contact email here. This technical notice does not replace legal advice for your specific business, jurisdictions or campaign.

What we collect

  • Waitlist: email address, marketing-consent version, source/UTM data and signup timestamp.
  • Security: limited technical data used by Firebase and bot-protection services to operate and protect the form.
  • Optional analytics: after you actively accept analytics, Google Analytics may collect usage and device information for aggregated measurement. Advertising and personalised Google signals are disabled in this implementation.

Why we use it

  • To operate the waitlist and send the product updates you requested.
  • To prevent abuse, duplicates and fraudulent submissions.
  • Only with analytics consent, to understand aggregate website use and improve the site.

For EU/UK visitors, marketing and analytics are consent-based. Consent is optional, specific and can be withdrawn at any time through Cookie settings in the site footer. Refusing analytics does not affect access to the site.

Cookies and controls

Essential storage is used only to remember your analytics choice. Google Analytics is not loaded until you choose Accept analytics. You can choose Decline analytics with equal prominence, or change the decision later from the footer. The analytics-consent choice is stored for up to 180 days.

Sharing and international transfers

Waitlist records are stored in private Google Firebase/Firestore infrastructure. If analytics is accepted, Google Analytics processes analytics information. Google may process data outside your country; review Google’s applicable data-processing and transfer terms before public launch.

Retention

Keep waitlist information only for the pre-launch and launch communication purpose, then delete or anonymise it according to a documented retention schedule. Delete records sooner when a valid deletion request applies.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or obtain a copy of personal information, withdraw consent and object to or restrict certain processing. California residents may also have rights to know, delete, correct and opt out of a sale or sharing of personal information where applicable. AirGap Paste does not sell or share waitlist data for cross-context behavioural advertising.

How to contact us

For the current prototype stage, reply to a future AirGap Paste email to request access or deletion. Replace this section with a direct, monitored privacy-contact email and legal-controller details before collecting waitlist registrations publicly.
